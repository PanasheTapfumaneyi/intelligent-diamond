Who we are?

Intelligent Diamond is a consulting and technology company that provides digital transformation services to enterprise clients.

We are a team of experienced professionals with a passion for innovation and a vision for a future where technology and human creativity converge to create unique and bespoke solutions that transform industries.

We service clients in Africa and Europe through our offices in Mauritius, South Africa and Finland.

We help our clients achieve efficiency, growth, and agility in their operations with the help of our tailor-made and innovative, AI-powered solutions. We specialise in the following areas:

Project Management

Operating model and Centre of Excellence design and deployment

Customised solution design, development, and deployment

Process re-engineering and optimisation (Lean)

Identification of innovative and intelligent use cases

Digital transformation strategy and implementation
